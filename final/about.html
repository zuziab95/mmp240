About Us

8 Things You Should Know About God's Food

1. God's Food was founded in 2018 to help people find great local restaurants.
2. God's Food had a monthly average of 1 million unique visitors.
3. God's Food have written more than 155 million reviews.
4. In addition to reviews, you can use God's Food to find events, lists and to talk with other users.
5. God's Food makes money by selling ads to local businesses.
6. God's Food uses automated software to recommend the most helpful and reliable reviews for the God's Food community among the millions we get. The software looks at dozens of different signals, including various measures of quality, reliability, and activity on God's Food. The process has nothing to do with whether a business advertises on God's Food or not.
7. You can access God's Food via iPhone, Android, and more.
8. The Local God's Food brings locals updates on the latest and greatest business openings & other happenings.
